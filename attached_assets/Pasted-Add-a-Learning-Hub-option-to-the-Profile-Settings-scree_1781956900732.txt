## 5. DICTIONARY PAGE (app/learning/dictionary.tsx)
- Show an alphabetically organized list of betting terms
- Add a search bar at the top to filter terms
- Include these terms (and expand with more using your initiative):

  - Accumulator: A bet combining multiple selections where all must win
  - Arbitrage: Betting on all outcomes across different bookmakers for guaranteed profit
  - Asian Handicap: A handicap system that eliminates the draw option
  - Bankroll: Total money set aside for betting
  - Bettor: A person who places bets
  - Bookmaker: A company that accepts and pays out bets
  - Cash Out: Settling a bet before the event ends
  - Correct Score: Predicting the exact final score
  - Dead Heat: Two or more selections finishing equal
  - Double: A bet combining 2 selections
  - Draw No Bet: Stake refunded if match ends in a draw
  - Each Way: A bet split between win and place
  - Expected Value (EV): Long-term average return of a bet
  - Fixed Odds: Odds agreed at time of placing bet
  - Form: Recent performance history of a team/player
  - Handicap: Virtual advantage/disadvantage given to a team
  - Hedging: Placing additional bets to reduce risk
  - In-Play: Betting on events while they are happening
  - Juice/Vig: Bookmaker's commission built into odds
  - Kelly Criterion: Formula for optimal bet sizing
  - Lay Bet: Betting against an outcome on an exchange
  - Lucky 15: 4 selections — 15 bets total
  - Market: A specific betting option offered by a bookmaker
  - Matched Betting: Using free bets to guarantee profit
  - Minimum Stake: Lowest amount allowed per bet
  - Multiples: Bets with more than one selection
  - No Bet: Bet voided and stake returned
  - Odds: Numerical expression of likelihood and payout
  - Odds-on: When odds suggest outcome is more likely than not
  - Over/Under: Betting on total being above or below a set number
  - Patent: 7 bets from 3 selections
  - Payout: Amount received when a bet wins
  - Price: Another word for odds
  - Profit: Winnings minus the original stake
  - Push: When a bet results in a tie — stake returned
  - Return: Total amount received back including stake
  - ROI: Return on Investment
  - Selection: The outcome you are betting on
  - Sharp: A professional, sophisticated bettor
  - Singles: A bet on one selection only
  - Stake: The amount wagered
  - System Bet: Multiple bets covering combinations of selections
  - Treble: A bet combining 3 selections
  - True Odds: Real probability of an outcome without bookmaker margin
  - Value Bet: A bet where odds are higher than true probability
  - Void Bet: A cancelled bet with stake returned
  - Wager: Another word for a bet
  - Yankee: 11 bets from 4 selections

- When a term is tapped, open a detail modal or screen showing:
  - Term name (large, bold, gold color)
  - Full definition
  - A practical example of how it is used
  - A "Used in a sentence" section
  - Related terms listed at the bottom

- The dictionary must have smooth navigation and look premium
